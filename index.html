About Me

Amanda has a BA in English and Art, an MA in Theater and Performance Studies, and currently works in a pharmacy so, naturally, she thought learning full-stack development would be the next logical step.

Honestly though, she knows design, creativity, and how to finish a project. She can think on her feet and likes building on the works of others both collaboratively and individually. She thinks most of these skills are transferable to coding, and she hopes to build more skills along the way.

In her spare time, Amanda likes knitting, painting, playing with dogs, and boxing. She likes to work with her hands and to read long novels. Unsuprisingly, Herman Melville's Moby-Dick is her favourite book. Her top five favourite bands are Stars, New Order, The Decemberists, David Bowie, and Fleet Foxes. Her top five favourite foods are tacos, pizza, cannoli, bread, and tacos (at the moment, y'all, because she really loves food of all kinds). Sometimes she uses British spellings because she lived in England for two years. One day she wants to have a house and a wife and a yard so she can actually garden.

Amanda thinks she will be most interested in Front End Development, but she is very intrigued by the mysteries of the Back End. Her dad works in software development, so naturally he is pleased as punch that she's taking an interest in his world. Though, he does wish she was learning Python.

Mostly, Amanda wants to always keep learning and growing. And also to get really really good at coding.
